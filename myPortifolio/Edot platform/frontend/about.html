About EDOT

Learn about our mission, vision, and the problems we solve.

What is EDOT?

EDOT (Education Digital Online Tutorials) is a platform designed for primary and secondary school students, offering both online learning and in-person tutoring to make education more accessible and effective.

Our Mission

To empower students with quality education, personalized tutoring, and technology-driven tools that make learning engaging and practical.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital learning platform in Ethiopia and beyond, bridging the gap between students and expert tutors.

The Problem We Solve

Many students lack access to quality educational resources and expert guidance. EDOT provides affordable and accessible learning solutions to help every student reach their full potential.

Our Goals

  • Provide a flexible learning experience.
  • Offer expert-led tutorials in various subjects.
  • Make education affordable for all.
  • Integrate digital tools for effective study.
