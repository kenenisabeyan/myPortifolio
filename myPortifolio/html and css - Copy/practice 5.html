Welcome to EDOT Platform

Education Digital Online Tutorials (EDOT) is a digital learning solution for urban, semi-urban, and rural students in Ethiopia. Our goal is to make quality education accessible and affordable.

📖 About EDOT

EDOT is designed to address common educational challenges such as:

  • Lack of enough tutorials
  • Poor quality of educational resources
  • Unemployment and underutilization of teachers
  • Lack of family engagement in learning

🎯 Objectives

Our goal is to improve the quality of education through digital means.

💡 Our Solutions

  • Personalized Learning – Students learn at their own pace
  • Instant Support – Ask questions and get instant answers
  • Past Exam Practice – Work on real past year questions
  • Skilled Teacher Network – Access to qualified instructors

🌟 Why EDOT is Unique

  • Serves urban, semi-urban, and rural communities with access to electricity and devices
  • High quality at low cost
  • Accepts user feedback
  • Affordable, accurate, and accessible

Key Activities

  • Platform development
  • Teacher registration
  • Student registration
  • Service promotion and outreach

Key Metrics

  • Progress and retention rates
  • Engagement and session completion
